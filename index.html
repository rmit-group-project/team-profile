Group project

A simplified and less expensive home automation system that is capable of controlling lights, heaters, tv, radios etc by touch, sounds or eye and body motion's for people with disabilities and anybody who desires a simple solution. Today these things are available but are very expensive and require specialists trades like electricians and builders to install or electronic whizzes to build. Other devices like Google home or Amazon Alexa can be used but won't connect to or operate all devices and settings correctly requiring multiple different home automation systems. My project will be an electronic device operated by a native unit or mobile phone and computer application using eye and body motion, sound or touch to control which can operate all these devices and their functions and using the power of AI to improve the user experience.
